Klosters is a charming alpine village located in the heart of Switzerland, surrounded by scenic mountains and stunning natural beauty. As a popular ski resort, it attracts tourists from all over the world every year due to its excellent ski slopes and adventurous winter activities. Apart from skiing, tourists can indulge in other winter attractions such as snowshoeing, ice skating, and tobogganing. During summers, Klosters transforms into a hiking paradise with numerous trails that offer breathtaking vistas of the Swiss Alps. The village also boasts of excellent restaurants, hotels, and shops, where tourists can relax, dine, and shop for souvenirs.
