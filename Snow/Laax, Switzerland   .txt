As a tourist in Laax, Switzerland, you'll find yourself surrounded by breathtaking natural landscapes and jaw-dropping mountain views. This charming Alpine village is known for its world-class skiing and snowboarding, with plenty of well-groomed slopes to explore. However, Laax also offers plenty of summer activities, such as biking, hiking, and lake sports. The town itself boasts a lively atmosphere, with plenty of restaurants, bars, and shops to explore. Don't miss out on visiting the Laaxersee, a stunning alpine lake. Overall, Laax is the perfect destination for both adrenaline junkies and those looking for a relaxing outdoor escape.
