Val di Fassa is a picturesque Alpine valley in the north-east of Italy's Trentino region, surrounded by the towering Dolomite Mountains. It offers visitors breath-taking views of the rugged mountain landscape, as well as a wide range of outdoor activities. The area is renowned for its skiing, with several world-renowned ski resorts located within close proximity, but also for hiking, biking, and climbing in the summer months. The valley is dotted with charming towns and villages, where visitors can enjoy local cuisine, traditions and culture. A visit to Val di Fassa is a journey through a magical land of natural beauty and outdoor adventure.
