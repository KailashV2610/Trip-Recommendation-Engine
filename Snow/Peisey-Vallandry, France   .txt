Peisey-Vallandry is a charming French mountain village, nestled in the heart of the Tarentaise Valley. The village boasts stunning alpine scenery, with several picturesque ski slopes and mountain trails for skiing, snowboarding, and hiking, making it the perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The quaint village center is lined with cozy restaurants, cafes, and bars, offering mouth-watering local cuisine and drinks. The historic 17th-century chapels, the Baroque architecture, and the traditional chalets add to the village's charm. Overall, Peisey-Vallandry is a delightful and peaceful retreat, offering visitors a chance to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse themselves in the serene beauty of the French Alps.
