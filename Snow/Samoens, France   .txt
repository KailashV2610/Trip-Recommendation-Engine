Samoens, France is a charming alpine village in the Haute-Savoie region of the French Alps. As a tourist, you will immediately be struck by the traditional architecture, with its wooden chalets and slate roofs, that gives the village a warm and rustic feel. In the summer, the area is popular for hiking and mountain biking, with numerous trails leading up into the surrounding mountains. The village is also well-known for its natural waterfalls and lush forests, offering a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. In the winter, Samoens is a popular ski resort, with access to the Grand Massif ski area and over 265km of slopes.
