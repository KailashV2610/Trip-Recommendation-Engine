Kiruna is a small town located in the northern region of Sweden, known for its stunning natural beauty and rich local culture. As a tourist, you will be mesmerized by the towering mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and pristine forests that surround the town. In wintertime, you can enjoy skiing, snowmobiling, and other winter sports. You can experience the northern lights, or Aurora Borealis, which usually appears between September and April. Kiruna is also home to the famous Icehotel, a unique lodging option made entirely of ice and snow. Don't forget to try the local cuisine, which includes reindeer meat and Arctic berries.
