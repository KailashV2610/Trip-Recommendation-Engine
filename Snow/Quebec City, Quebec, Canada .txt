Quebec City is a charming and historic destination, boasting cobbled streets with charming cafes, shops, and restaurants. The UNESCO World Heritage Site of Old Quebec is the city's crown jewel, with handsome stone buildings, museums, and landmarks like the awe-inspiring Chateau Frontenac. From the bustling Petit Champlain district with its unique boutiques and galleries to the historic Place Royale with its small cafes, visitors can indulge in local delicacies like maple syrup and poutine. The city hosts events throughout the year, including the Winter Carnival, and visitors will be enchanted by the colorful architecture, lively culture, and scenic beauty of the St. Lawrence River.
