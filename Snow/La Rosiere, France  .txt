La Rosiere is a charming ski resort located in southeastern France, close to the Italian border. As a tourist, you will be enchanted by the serene beauty of this winter wonderland surrounded by stunning Alpine scenery. The resort offers an impressive range of skiing and snowboarding opportunities, with over 160 kilometers of slopes and 37 lifts. The pistes are well-maintained, catering to beginners and experienced skiers alike. When you take a break from the slopes, you can enjoy the local cuisine and indulge in delicious French delicacies. The cozy atmosphere of La Rosiere makes it an ideal destination for a family-friendly winter holiday.
