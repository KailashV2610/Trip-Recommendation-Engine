Big Sky Motel is currently the newest luxury studio and apartment accommodation facility in Omarama. The tranquil setting in a quiet street provides serenity and calmness as you take in the mountain views. The complex has studio and 1 bedroom units each with superior decor including a tiled ensuite, fully equipped kitchen, free WiFi, USB charge points, Smart TV, and air conditioning. A wheelchair accessible unit is available with carers room adjoining if required. A laundry with dryer, outdoor terrace and BBQ area, off-street parking, and rubbish recycling station adds to the uniqueness of the property
