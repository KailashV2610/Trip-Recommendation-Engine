Jasper, Alberta is a beautiful destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts alike. The town is surrounded by stunning mountain ranges, turquoise lakes, and abundant wildlife including bears, elk, and mountain goats. It's a great place for hiking, biking, and skiing in the winter months. Visitors can also take a scenic drive on the Icefields Parkway, explore the glaciers at the Columbia Icefield, or take a dip in the natural hot springs. The town itself is small yet charming with plenty of restaurants, souvenir shops, and unique accommodations like log cabins and mountain lodges. Overall, Jasper offers a true Canadian Rockies experience.
