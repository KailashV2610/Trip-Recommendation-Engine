Portes du Soleil is a picturesque holiday destination located amidst the mountains in France and Switzerland. It's a perfect place for skiing, mountain biking, hiking, and sightseeing. The region boasts of several charming Alpine villages, each with its unique character and culture. The friendly locals and breathtaking surroundings make it a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. With over 650 km of ski slopes, it's a paradise for skiing enthusiasts, while the summer months offer plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures. The region's rich history and cultural heritage offer several opportunities to explore museums, exhibitions, and local food and beverages.
