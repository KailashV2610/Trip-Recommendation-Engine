On the mountainside, beneath the sun of the Southern Alps, shelters this beautiful renovated Club Med Village : a cascade of rooftops nestling in the powder. Les Deux-Alpes invites snow-sports aficionados to launch themselves into an exceptional ski domain, overlooked by the Meije glacier, a true emblem of this region.
