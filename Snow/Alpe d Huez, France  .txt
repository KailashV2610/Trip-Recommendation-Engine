Alpe d'Huez is a picturesque ski resort in the French Alps, located about 1860 meters above sea level. The panoramic mountain views and the crisp mountain air are mesmerizing. It is known for its impressive ski slopes, ranging from beginners' to expert skiers. The resort is also famous for its terrain park, snow biking, tobogganing, and snowshoeing. During the summer, tourists can engage in mountain biking, hiking, and rock climbing. The town center is lively, buzzing with activity in bars, restaurants, shops, and markets. With endless activities and breathtaking surroundings, Alpe d'Huez is an alluring destination any time of the year.
