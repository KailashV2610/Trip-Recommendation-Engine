Great Barrier Reef, Australia is a breathtaking natural wonder that offers endless opportunities for adventure and exploration. The world's largest coral reef system spans over 2,000 kilometers along the Queensland coast, boasting colourful coral formations and an array of marine life. As a tourist, you can explore the reef with a guided snorkelling or scuba diving tour, or take a scenic flight to admire its beauty from above. The region is also home to a variety of islands, national parks, and charming coastal towns that offer unique experiences, from hiking through lush rainforest to indulging in fresh seafood cuisine.
