Picos de Europa is a stunning national park in northern Spain, offering a range of outdoor activities and breathtaking scenery. Towering peaks, rugged cliffs, lush forests, and crystal-clear lakes provide endless opportunities for hiking, climbing, and wildlife watching. At the heart of the park is the magnificent Naranjo de Bulnes, a dramatic peak that attracts serious climbers from around the world. The charming villages in the park provide a glimpse into local life and offer delicious traditional cuisine. With its pristine beauty and diverse landscapes, Picos de Europa is a must-see destination for any nature lover.
