Plitvice Lakes National Park is a stunning sanctuary of emerald lakes, cascading waterfalls, and verdant forests. As a tourist, you will be awed by the turquoise waters of the 16 interconnected lakes, which are formed by natural dams of moss-covered rocks. The trails leading through the park offer breathtaking views at every turn, while the sound of falling water and birdsong create a peaceful atmosphere. Visitors can explore on foot or by boat, taking in the beauty of the park's Upper and Lower Lakes, as well as its many waterfalls, including the 78-meter-high Veliki Slap. Overall, Plitvice Lakes is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.
