Ayers Rock, also known as Uluru, is a breathtaking natural wonder located in the heart of the Australian Outback. As a tourist, you will be hypnotized by its towering red rock formations, stunning sunsets and stunning starry skies. Ayers Rock is owned by the Anangu people, who have lived on this land for thousands of years and have a rich cultural history. Visitors can learn about their Indigenous heritage and spiritual beliefs through guided tours, walks and cultural experiences. It's a must-see destination for anyone interested in immersing themselves in unique Australian and Indigenous culture.
