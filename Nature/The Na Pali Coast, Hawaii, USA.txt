The Na Pali Coast of Hawaii is a breathtakingly beautiful wonder of nature that leaves one spellbound. The dramatic cliffs, lush greenery, and shimmering blue waters make for a stunning view. As a tourist, one can marvel at the diverse marine life and also go snorkelling or diving to catch a glimpse of turtles, dolphins, manta rays, and more. The coastline also has numerous hiking trails that one can explore, offering stunning vistas from atop the mountains. One can also take a boat or helicopter tour to witness the magical sunset or sunrise that casts a beautiful hue over the entire landscape.
