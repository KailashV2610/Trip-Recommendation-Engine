Salar de Uyuni is an otherworldly salt flat in Bolivia, a breathtaking natural wonder that is as mesmerizing as it is vast. As a tourist, you can witness the flat's vast expanse that stretches for miles and miles, forming a seemingly endless white horizon line. You can see the beautiful hexagonal salt crust that covers the ground, and experience the unique perspective of feeling like you're walking on water. Along the way, you'll discover the area's diverse flora, fauna, pink flamingos, as well as a scattering of small villages, each with their own distinct culture and character. Whether you're here for adventure or relaxation, Salar de Uyuni offers an unforgettable experience.
