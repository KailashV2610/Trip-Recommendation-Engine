Death Valley National Park is a must-visit destination for adventurous tourists. The park is located in California, USA, and covers a vast expanse of surreal landscape, including sand dunes, salt flats, and canyons. The high temperatures make it the hottest and driest place in North America. You can explore the stunning scenery, hike the rugged terrain of the Panamint Mountains or take a scenic drive on Badwater Road, which winds through some of the park's most impressive sights. The park also provides an opportunity to view unique wildlife, including coyotes, bighorn sheep, and roadrunners.
