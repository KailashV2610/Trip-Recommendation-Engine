Machu Picchu is an ancient Incan city located in Peru. It is situated high in the Andes Mountains and was built around 1450 AD. The site is surrounded by breathtaking views of lush forests, steep cliffs, and the winding Urubamba River. As a tourist, you will be amazed by the intricate stonework and architecture of the city, as well as the sacred temples and agricultural terraces. You can also hike up Huayna Picchu for panoramic views or visit the Machu Picchu Museum to learn about the history of the site. This magical place is a must-see destination for any traveler to Peru.
