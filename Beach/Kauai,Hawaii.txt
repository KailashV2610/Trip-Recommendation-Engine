A sensory wallop of lush vegetation, high-drama cliffs, and aquamarine waters With comparatively few commercial developments, "The Garden Isle" has preserved its pristine beauty and laid-back Hawaiian spirit. Waterfalls, scenic drives, and more than 60 beaches dot the landscape offering snorkeling, water sports, and shallow tidal pools that kids enjoy. Explore the island's natural beauty with a hike on the the Kalalau Trail along the Na Pali coastline, take a day trip to Waimea Canyon, or experience life on a sugar plantation at Grove Farm Homestead Museum.
