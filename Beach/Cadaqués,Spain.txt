Cadaqués is a charming seaside town located on the northeastern coast of Spain, in the Catalonia region. The town is known for its beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters and picturesque white-washed houses which are scattered along the coast. Cadaqués has a vibrant art scene with numerous galleries, shops and museums celebrating the works of both local and international artists. Strolling through the narrow streets of the old town, visitors can discover a variety of eclectic shops, cafes and restaurants offering traditional Spanish cuisine. Apart from enjoying the scenic beauty of the town, tourists can also explore the nearby Cap de Creus National Park, which boasts stunning hiking trails and breathtaking views of the sea.
