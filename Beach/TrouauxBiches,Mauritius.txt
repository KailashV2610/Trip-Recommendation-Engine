Trou aux Biches is an idyllic coastal village located in Mauritius that is a popular tourist spot. The beaches are stunning with crystal-clear waters, powdery white sands and are perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and water sports. The reef shelters the bay, creating calm and shallow lagoons ideal for snorkelling and watching marine life. The village is lined with restaurants and bars serving fresh seafood, and there are plenty of shops offering local handicrafts and souvenirs. For those who love adventure, there are various activities such as hiking, cycling, and zip-lining available. Overall, Trou aux Biches has something for everyone, making it a perfect holiday destination.
