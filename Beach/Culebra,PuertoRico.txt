Culebra, Puerto Rico is a breathtakingly beautiful destination that offers a true tropical paradise experience. The island is surrounded by turquoise waters, white sand beaches, and lush vegetation. The world-famous Flamenco Beach is a must-visit, with crystal clear water and a horseshoe-shaped bay. Apart from beaches, Culebra is also home to stunning natural reserves, including the Culebra National Wildlife reserve with over 50 species of coral reefs and over 200 species of fish. The island is easy to explore with rentals readily available, from ATVs to kayaks, boats, and snorkeling gear. With its laid-back atmosphere and stunning scenery, Culebra is a fantastic choice for those seeking a peaceful tropical getaway.
