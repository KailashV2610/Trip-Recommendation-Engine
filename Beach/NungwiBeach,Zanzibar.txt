Nungwi Beach in Zanzibar is a paradise for tourists seeking solace in sun, sand, and sea. The white sandy beach strewn with coconut palms and crystal-clear water is the perfect escape for those looking to disconnect from the world. The beach is lined with resorts and restaurants catering to every need of the tourists. At night, the beach comes alive with music and bonfires. Being the prime spot for water sports, one can indulge in snorkeling, diving, jet skiing, and deep-sea fishing. The famous Nungwi Village with its traditional dhow-building factories is an attraction in itself. Nungwi Beach, Zanzibar, is a perfect mix of adventure, relaxation, and culture.
