Jaco is a vibrant coastal town in Costa Rica that is perfect for tourists seeking a thrilling beach vacation. Known for its incredible waves, Jaco is a surfer's paradise, but it's not only watersports that are popular here, as there are plenty of other thrilling activities such as ATV tours and zipline adventures. The town also boasts of several restaurants serving delicious local cuisine, bars and nightclubs for those who want to have a good time after the sunsets. With its beautiful beaches, breathtaking sunsets, and welcoming people, Jaco is the perfect destination for travelers wanting to experience the Pura Vida lifestyle.
