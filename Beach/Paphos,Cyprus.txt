Paphos, Cyprus is a charming coastal city that offers the perfect mix of history, culture and natural beauty. As a tourist, you'll be captivated by the ancient ruins and architectural marvels, such as the House of Dionysus and the Paphos Archaeological Park. Additionally, the city's picturesque beaches, crystal-clear waters and stunning sunsets are bound to take your breath away. The charming harbor is a bustling hub of activity during the day, with plenty of traditional tavernas, souvenir shops and cafes to explore. At night, Paphos transforms into the ultimate party destination, with numerous nightclubs and bars to suit all tastes.
