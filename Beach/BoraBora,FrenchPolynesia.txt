BoraBora is a little paradise island located in French Polynesia, it is a place that is like no other. With its crystal clear blue waters, pristine white-sand beaches, and picturesque palm trees, it's no wonder why it's known as one of the most beautiful places on earth. BoraBora also has rich cultural heritage that is displayed in the local art and dance performances. You can explore the island's stunning coral reefs while snorkeling or scuba diving or simply relax on the beach with a refreshing cocktail. The resorts on the island also offer an array of water activities such as Jet Skiing, paddle boarding, and parasailing.
