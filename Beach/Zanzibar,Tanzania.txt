Zanzibar, Tanzania is a tropical paradise nestled in the Indian Ocean. As a tourist, you can expect crystal clear turquoise waters, powder soft white sand beaches, coral reefs, and pristine marine life. You can take a spice tour and indulge your senses with the exotic aromas and flavors of fresh spices. Visit the historical Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage site crowded with intricate labyrinthine narrow streets, delightful shops, cafes, markets, and colorful old buildings. The island boasts of an irresistible mix of cultures that reflect in its food, art, and music. Zanzibar is the perfect place to relax and unwind while exploring the rich history and culture of Tanzania.
