Saint Martin, located in the Caribbean, is a tropical paradise for tourists seeking sun, sand and sea. It is the smallest landmass in the world to be divided between two countries, with the northern half belonging to France and the southern half to the Netherlands. The island boasts pristine white sand beaches, turquoise waters, and a lush green landscape. Visitors can engage in a range of water sports activities, such as scuba diving, snorkeling, kayaking, and sailing. Additionally, the island offers a rich cultural experience with a variety of cuisines, music, and art. It's the perfect destination for a Caribbean getaway.
