Palawan, Philippines is a must-visit destination for any traveler seeking pristine natural surroundings and a laid-back atmosphere. This island province boasts stunning white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, lush jungles, and towering limestone cliffs. The local culture and history are rich and diverse, with traditional communities and remnants of Spanish colonial architecture. Visitors can go island-hopping, snorkeling or diving in some of the most diverse marine life areas in the world, explore the world-famous underground river and hike to hidden waterfalls. With its breathtaking scenery and warm hospitality, Palawan is the perfect place to relax and immerse yourself in nature.
