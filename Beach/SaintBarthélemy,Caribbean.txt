Saint Barthélemy, also known as St. Barts, is a small island paradise in the Caribbean. It boasts pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters, picture-perfect scenery, and a relaxed and luxurious atmosphere. From the bustling capital Gustavia to the charming fishing village of Corossol, this island has something for everyone. Visitors can enjoy fine dining, shopping at high-end boutiques or exploring the unique flora and fauna, including the famous pink iguanas. Charming villas and luxury resorts offer a variety of accommodations, with some of the world's most beautiful views. Saint Barthélemy is a must-see destination for any tropical tourist!
