Sanur is a charming beachside town on Bali's southeast coast. As a tourist, you will immediately notice the tranquil and relaxing atmosphere. The beach is picturesque with its white sand, blue waters, and palm trees swaying in the breeze. Sanur offers plenty of recreational activities such as water sports and cycling. There are a plethora of restaurants and cafes along the beach promenade serving traditional Indonesian food and international cuisine. The town also has several interesting museums and cultural centers showcasing Indonesian art and history. Sanur provides a perfect balance of relaxation and exploration, making it a popular destination for tourists seeking a laid-back and enjoyable holiday.
