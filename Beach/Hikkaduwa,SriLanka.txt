Hikkaduwa,SriLanka is a charming coastal town located along the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka. Known for its stunning golden sand beaches, crystal clear waters, and lush greenery, it is a popular destination for tourists seeking a relaxing beach escape. The town offers a range of activities such as surfing, snorkeling, and scuba diving to explore the underwater world throbbing with colorful life. It is also famous for its vibrant nightlife, bustling markets, and mouth-watering cuisine, including traditional seafood curries, hoppers and roti. Overall, Hikkaduwa is the perfect destination for anyone seeking an unforgettable beach vacation in Sri Lanka.
