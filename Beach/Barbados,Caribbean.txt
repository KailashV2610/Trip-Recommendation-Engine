Barbados, Caribbean is a tropical paradise for tourists seeking sun, sand and relaxation. This charming island boasts of crystal clear turquoise waters, world-class beaches, lush green forests, and a rich cultural heritage. Visitors can explore the picturesque towns and villages, enjoy the local cuisine and get acquainted with the friendly locals. Tourists can engage in water sports such as surfing, snorkelling, kayaking, and scuba diving. With a vibrant nightlife, numerous historical landmarks and museums, and a diverse range of art and crafts shops, there is never a dull moment in Barbados. A perfect holiday destination that captures the essence of the Caribbean.
