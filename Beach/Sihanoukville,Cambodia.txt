Sihanoukville, located in the southern part of Cambodia, is a coastal city known for its beautiful white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters that attract both locals and tourists. Apart from lazing on the beach, visitors can indulge in various water sports activities such as snorkeling, diving, and paddle-boarding. The town also offers a variety of restaurants and bars serving delicious Cambodian delicacies and drinks throughout the day and night. Sihanoukville also has several historical sites and cultural landmarks such as temples and museums that offer a glimpse into the country's history and traditions. It is also the perfect gateway to explore the nearby islands such as Koh Rong and Koh Rong Samloem.
