Newport Beach is a charming coastal city located in Orange County, California, perfect for tourists seeking a relaxed yet chic vacation destination. With its stunning beaches and picturesque harbor, Newport Beach is renowned for its water-based activities such as surfing, paddle-boarding, and sailing. For those seeking a more upscale experience, the city offers high-end boutiques, restaurants and bars, and luxurious beachfront estates. Visit the iconic Balboa Island, stroll along the pier at Newport Beach, or take a sunset sail on Newport Harbor. With a mild Mediterranean climate year-round, this city is a must-visit for all those in search of a quintessential Californian beach vacation.
