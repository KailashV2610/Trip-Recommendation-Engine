Paje, Tanzania is a picturesque coastal village located on the southeastern coast of Zanzibar. It is renowned for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, white sandy beaches and a laid-back atmosphere. The village offers a range of water sports activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, and kite-surfing. It is also home to one of the largest coral reefs in the world, making it an ideal destination for marine lovers. The village offers a wide range of accommodation from simple beach huts to luxury resorts, all surrounded by palm trees and lush greenery. Paje offers a unique taste of the Swahili culture through its food, music, and traditional dances.
