Biarritz, situated on the southwestern coast of France, is a stunning seaside town often associated with its long stretches of golden beaches, excellent surf spots and breathtaking ocean views. The town is a perfect mix of culture, history, and modern day facilities, with its picturesque villas, boutique shops and gourmet restaurants. Biarritz boasts a charming old town where visitors can explore the architecture, museums and art galleries. A popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, Biarritz offers a wide range of activities including hiking, cycling, golfing and fishing. Any visitor will appreciate the rich Basque culture and warm hospitality that Biarritz has to offer.
