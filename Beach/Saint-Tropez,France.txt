Saint-Tropez is a beautiful and vibrant coastal town located in the south of France. Its charming cobblestone streets are lined with lovely shops, cafes and restaurants that offer traditional French cuisine. The town sits on a picturesque bay, filled with luxurious yachts and sun-kissed beaches. The iconic St-Tropez pink house overlooking the port is a popular spot for taking pictures. Visitors can explore the historic La Citadelle and the nearby art galleries, museums and wineries. The nightlife is lively with plenty of bars, clubs and live music venues. Whether you love culture, history, beach, or party scene, Saint-Tropez has something for everyone.
