Gold Coast, located in Queensland, Australia, is a major tourist destination that attracts visitors from all over the world. Known for its pristine beaches, lush hinterland, and vibrant nightlife, this coastal city offers a range of activities for every type of tourist. Whether it's surfing, hiking, shopping, or exploring its famous theme parks, there's always something to do in Gold Coast. The city is also home to a thriving arts and culture scene, as well as world-class dining and accommodation options. With a warm climate and friendly locals, Gold Coast is the perfect destination for a beach holiday, family vacation, or weekend getaway.
