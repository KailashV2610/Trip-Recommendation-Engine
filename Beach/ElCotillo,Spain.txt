ElCotillo is a charming fishing village located on the western coast of Fuerteventura, Spain. Known for its picturesque beaches with turquoise waters, this town is a paradise for tourists who enjoy surfing, swimming, and sunbathing. The town has a peaceful and laid-back vibe that is perfect for relaxing and unwinding. ElCotillo's rugged coastline features stunning natural rock formations that are a must-see attraction for adventurous travelers. The town's restaurants and cafes serve delicious Spanish cuisine, seafood, and local wines that will tantalize your taste buds. Overall, ElCotillo is a hidden gem that is perfect for a tranquil holiday away from the hustle and bustle of modern life.
