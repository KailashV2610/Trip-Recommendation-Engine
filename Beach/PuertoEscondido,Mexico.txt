Puerto Escondido is a beach paradise located on the Pacific coast of Oaxaca, Mexico. As a tourist, you will be captivated by the warm and friendly locals, the tranquil and clear waters, and the vibrant atmosphere of the town. You will find a range of activities such as surfing, sunbathing, fishing and nightlife options. With pristine beaches, breathtaking sunsets, and delicious seafood, Puerto Escondido is a breathtaking destination that will leave you wanting more. Don't miss a chance to try the famous 'bioluminescent' phenomenon which is a unique natural wonder where the beach sparkles at night creating a magical experience!
