Rio Grande, Puerto Rico is a beautiful coastal town located on the northeast coast of the island. Known for its stunning beaches and lush tropical forests, this charming town offers an array of outdoor activities and cultural experiences for tourists. Visitors can enjoy swimming, surfing, and kayaking in the crystal-clear waters of the Caribbean Sea, or hike through the El Yunque Rainforest, the only tropical forest in the United States National Forest System. The town also boasts historic landmarks, local markets, and delicious cuisine. With its pleasant weather, laid-back atmosphere, and friendly locals, Rio Grande is a must-visit destination for any Puerto Rico tourist.
