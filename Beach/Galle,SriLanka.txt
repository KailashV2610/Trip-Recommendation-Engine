Galle in Sri Lanka is a picturesque coastal city that offers a blend of colonial and traditional Sri Lankan culture. The city is surrounded by majestic walls and is home to a historic Dutch Fort. The streets are lined with charming cafes, boutique shops, and galleries. The food scene is diverse, and you can enjoy fresh seafood, traditional Sri Lankan curries, and lots of tropical fruits. Don't miss the opportunity to visit the Galle Lighthouse or enjoy a stroll along Unawatuna Beach. In summary, Galle is a beautiful destination that offers culture, history, and stunning coastal scenery.
