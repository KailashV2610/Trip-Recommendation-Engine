San Sebastian, Spain is a captivating coastal town situated on the Basque coast. The city boasts of stunning beaches, amazing culinary delights, and a vibrant nightlife. The clear waters of La Concha Beach and the impressive Mount Igeldo provide a perfect backdrop for a relaxing holiday. Visitors are spoilt for choice on where to eat as the city has the highest number of Michelin-starred restaurants per capita in the world. The old town, with its cobbled streets, narrow alleys, and historic buildings, provides a perfect opportunity to explore the city's rich history and culture. San Sebastian is an ideal holiday destination for travelers seeking a balance between relaxation and exploration.
