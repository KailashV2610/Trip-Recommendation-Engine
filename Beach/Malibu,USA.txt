It is well-known for its expansive and picturesque beaches with crystal clear waters and perfect waves for surfing, including Point Dume, Zuma Beach and Surfrider Beach. Beyond the beaches, Malibu also features stunning hiking trails, with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean and the Santa Monica Mountains. The city boasts of luxurious beachside homes, high-end restaurants, and trendy boutiques, making it a must-see destination for any travel enthusiast.
