Sanya, located on the southernmost tip of Hainan Island, is a tropical paradise with stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters and abundant sunshine. As a tourist, you can bask in the sun, take a dip in the sea, explore scenic spots such as the Nanshan Temple and enjoy delicious seafood. For a touch of luxury, visitors can check-in to one of the many five-star resorts where they'll enjoy world-class amenities and services. The city is also a hub for cultural exchanges, hosting many annual cultural tourism events. Sanya is the perfect destination for those looking for a laid-back vacation amidst natural beauty.
