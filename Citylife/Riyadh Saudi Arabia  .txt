Riyadh is the capital and largest city in Saudi Arabia, and is a vibrant and modern metropolis with a rich cultural heritage. As a tourist, you'll find a mix of traditional and contemporary influences, with stunning architecture, beautifully manicured parks, and plenty of shopping malls and modern entertainment options. The city is known for its hot and dry climate, but visitors can escape the heat by visiting indoor attractions such as museums and galleries. You can also explore the old town, known as Al-Diriyah, which is a UNESCO World Heritage site with ancient architecture and authentic local markets. Overall, Riyadh offers a fascinating blend of the old and new.
