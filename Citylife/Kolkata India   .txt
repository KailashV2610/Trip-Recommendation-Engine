Kolkata, the cultural capital of India, is a vibrant city with a unique blend of ancient and modern architecture. As a tourist, you'll be mesmerized by its bustling streets, teeming with people and bustling markets. Take a stroll through the maze of narrow lanes in the old city, and you'll find yourself lost in the bustling sounds and smells of street food, colorful saris, and colonial-era buildings. Visit the iconic Victoria Memorial, along with the Howrah Bridge and St. Paul's Cathedral, to experience Kolkata's rich cultural heritage. Treat your taste buds to an array of delicious Bengali cuisines, and embrace the city's friendly and welcoming way of life.
