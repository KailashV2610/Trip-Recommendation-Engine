Orlando, USA is a bustling and lively city that draws millions of tourists each year to its world-renowned theme parks, including Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, and SeaWorld. These attractions offer thrilling rides, entertaining shows, and opportunities to meet beloved characters from film and television. Away from the parks, visitors can enjoy a variety of shopping, dining, and entertainment options in areas like Universal CityWalk and Disney Springs. For those seeking outdoor adventures, Orlando boasts beautiful parks and lakes, as well as nearby opportunities for hiking, kayaking, and airboat rides through the Everglades. Overall, Orlando offers something for every type of tourist.
