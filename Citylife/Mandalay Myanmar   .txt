Mandalay, Myanmar is a vibrant and culturally rich city located in central Myanmar. The city is full of ancient palaces, temples and monasteries that showcase the country's rich heritage and history. The Shwenandaw Monastery and Kuthodaw Pagoda are popular attractions with their intricate wood carvings and towering stupa respectively. The Mandalay Hill is a great spot to catch the panoramic views of the cityscape. The local markets, especially the Zegyo Market, offer a glimpse into the everyday lives and culture of the locals. The city is bustling with street food vendors offering popular local delicacies like mohinga and tea leaf salad which visitors must try.
