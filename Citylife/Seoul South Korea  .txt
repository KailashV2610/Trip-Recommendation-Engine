Seoul, South Korea is a bustling city full of energy, culture, and history. As a tourist, you'll be impressed by the modern and sleek skyscrapers, alongside traditional architecture and ancient temples. The city is famously known for its delicious cuisine, including Korean BBQ, bibimbap, and kimchi. Shopping is a favorite pastime, with markets and malls offering everything from trendy fashion to unique handicrafts. Seoul is also steeped in history, with sites such as the Gyeongbokgung Palace, Bukchon Hanok Village and the Jeongdong Theater providing a glimpse into ancient traditions and customs. From nightlife to street food, there's always something exciting to discover in Seoul.
