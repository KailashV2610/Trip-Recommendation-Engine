Amman, Jordan is a bustling city that combines traditional Middle Eastern architecture with modern buildings, creating an interesting mix of old and new. The city is situated on several hills, providing stunning panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. As a tourist, you can marvel at the ancient ruins of the Citadel and Roman Theater, stroll through the busy streets of downtown and shop for souvenirs at the popular Rainbow Street. The city has a great food scene, offering delicious traditional Middle Eastern cuisine alongside international options. The people are friendly, welcoming and eager to share their culture and history with tourists.
