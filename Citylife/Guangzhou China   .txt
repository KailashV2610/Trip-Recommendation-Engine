Guangzhou, also known as Canton, is a thriving metropolis located in southern China. As a tourist, you will be delighted to discover a rich history and culture, delicious food, and impressive modern architecture. The city is home to numerous temples and museums that showcase the region's past, including the Chen Clan Ancestral Hall Museum and the Guangzhou Museum. You can also visit the Canton Tower, the third-tallest tower in the world, and enjoy stunning views of the city. The food scene in Guangzhou is a must-try, offering mouth-watering Cantonese cuisine that will leave you craving for more.
