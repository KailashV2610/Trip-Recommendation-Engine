Taichung, Taiwan is a bustling city located in the central part of the island that offers a unique blend of ancient and modern culture. As a tourist, you will find plenty of things to do and see in this vibrant city such as visiting the historic temples like the Paochueh Temple or exploring the colorful Rainbow Village. The city is also known for its delicious street food, bustling night markets, art museums, and beautiful parks like the famous Fengle Sculpture Park. The vibrant nightlife is also a major attraction for tourists as the city has plenty of bars, clubs, and restaurants to enjoy.
