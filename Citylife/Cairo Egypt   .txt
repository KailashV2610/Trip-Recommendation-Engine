Cairo, Egypt is a bustling and exciting city that is rich in history and culture. As a tourist, you will immediately notice the impressive pyramids and Sphinx of Giza, which are some of the world's most famous landmarks. In addition to these ancient wonders, Cairo also boasts many modern attractions, such as shopping centers, museums, and parks. The city is incredibly diverse and offers a wide range of cuisines, from traditional Middle Eastern fare to international dishes. Walking through the bustling streets, you will be immersed in vibrant colors and the heady scents of spice markets. Overall, Cairo is a fascinating destination that will leave a lasting impression on any traveler.
