Prague is a stunning city rich in history and culture. The winding cobblestone streets are lined with beautiful Baroque and Gothic architecture, and around every corner, there is a new surprise to uncover. The famous Charles Bridge is a must-see, with its stunning views of the Vltava River and historic landmarks on both sides. The Old Town Square is a bustling hub of activity, with street performers, musicians and mouth-watering food stalls. The city is also home to spectacular castles, churches and cathedrals, making it a perfect destination for history buffs. Overall, Prague is a vibrant and enchanting city, which will leave you wanting to come back for more.
