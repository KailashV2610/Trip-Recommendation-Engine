Da Nang is a vibrant coastal city in central Vietnam. It is a popular tourist destination with beautiful beaches, charming architecture, and a rich culture. The city is home to some of the best seafood restaurants in the country, which serve delicious local dishes like banh xeo and mi quang. Visitors can explore the ancient temples like the Linh Ung Pagoda and the Marble Mountains, or take a stroll along the Han River. The Ba Na Hills offer stunning views of the surrounding countryside and the famous Golden Bridge. Da Nang's warm weather and friendly locals complete the perfect tourist experience.
