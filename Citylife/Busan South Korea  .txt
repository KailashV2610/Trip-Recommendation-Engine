Busan is a bustling city in South Korea known for its beautiful beaches, rich cultural heritage, and tasty seafood. As a tourist, you can explore the vibrant markets, indulge in the delicious street food, visit museums and art galleries, hike the scenic trails, and relax on the stunning beaches. The city has something to offer for everyone, from historical landmarks like the Busan Tower and Beomeosa Temple to modern attractions like the Busan Cinema Center and Haeundae Beach. Busan is also known for its festivals, including the Busan Fireworks Festival, Busan International Film Festival, and Busan Sea Festival, ensuring there's always something exciting happening.
