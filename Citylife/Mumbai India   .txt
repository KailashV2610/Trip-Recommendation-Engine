Mumbai, also known as the "City of Dreams," is a bustling metropolis that never sleeps. It is a perfect blend of old world charm and modernism. The city is filled with numerous historical landmarks like the Gateway of India, the Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus, and the iconic Taj Mahal Palace Hotel. The beaches, including the famous Marine Drive, are breathtaking and offer a perfect location to relax and enjoy scenic views. The street food is delectable and an absolute must-try for foodies. The people are warm and hospitable, making the city more vibrant and exciting. All in all, Mumbai is an enchanting destination that leaves an unforgettable impression on visitors.
