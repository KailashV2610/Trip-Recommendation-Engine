Las Vegas is a city in the heart of Nevada known as the Entertainment Capital of the World. Its iconic, colorful skyline dazzles visitors and offers endless excitement with its casinos, luxury resorts, fine dining, and world-renowned entertainment scene. The city's main artery, the Las Vegas Strip, boasts numerous attractions from the Bellagio fountains to the Stratosphere Tower. The Neon Museum and Mob Museum showcase the city's rich history while the red rock canyons offer outdoor activities for nature enthusiasts. The city never sleeps and offers something for everyone making it a top tourist destination.
