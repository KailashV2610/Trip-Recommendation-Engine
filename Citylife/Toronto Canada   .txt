Toronto is a cosmopolitan city located on the shores of Lake Ontario. It offers a vibrant mix of cultures, attractions and experiences that make it a popular destination for visitors. The iconic CN Tower offers stunning views of the city and lake, while the modern and eclectic neighborhoods of Queen Street West and Kensington Market are perfect for strolling around and exploring local shops and eateries. The city's diverse population has also created a rich arts and cultural scene, with museums, galleries and theaters. Whether you're interested in sports, architecture, shopping, or simply soaking up the city's busy atmosphere, Toronto has something for everyone.
