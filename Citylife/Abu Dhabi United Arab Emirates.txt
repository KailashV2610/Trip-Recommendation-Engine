Abu Dhabi is a beautiful and multicultural city that offers a glimpse into the rich and fascinating culture of the United Arab Emirates. As a tourist, you will be awed by the stunning skyline and impressive architecture, especially at the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque; one of the largest mosques in the world. You can also explore the many museums, shopping malls, and entertainment venues located throughout the city. For outdoor enthusiasts, there is plenty to do, including visiting the beautiful beaches, traditional markets and the breath-taking Arabian desert. The city is also renowned for its luxurious hotels, restaurants, and vibrant nightlife scene.
