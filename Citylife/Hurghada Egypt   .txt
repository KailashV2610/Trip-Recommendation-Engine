Hurghada, Egypt is a beautiful seaside city that boasts crystal clear waters, sandy beaches, and sunny weather all year round. As a tourist, there is plenty to do and see in Hurghada, such as snorkeling or scuba diving in the Red Sea, visiting historic landmarks like the Hurghada Grand Mosque or the Coptic Church, or taking a desert safari or camel ride. The city also offers a wide range of dining options, from local Egyptian cuisine to international dishes. Despite the occasional hustle and bustle, Hurghada remains a relatively quiet city, perfect for relaxation and enjoying the scenic surroundings.
