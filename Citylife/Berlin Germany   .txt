Berlin is a vibrant and diverse city, offering a mix of rich history, culture and modernity. The city is home to many iconic landmarks such as the Brandenburg Gate, Berlin Wall, Checkpoint Charlie and the Reichstag building. Visitors can indulge in the city's renowned art scene at the Museum Island or admire the stunning architecture of the Bauhaus buildings. Berlin is also famous for its food culture, with street food and Michelin-starred restaurants serving up a variety of dishes. With a bustling nightlife, green parks, and numerous events scattered throughout the year, Berlin is a destination that offers something for every type of traveler.
