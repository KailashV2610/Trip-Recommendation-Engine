Luxor is a captivating city located in Upper Egypt, offering an unforgettable journey through ancient history. As a tourist, you will be amazed by the monumental temples, incredible tombs, and remarkable architecture that showcases the ingenuity of the ancient Egyptians. The highlight of the city is the Valley of the Kings, where the tombs of pharaohs and nobles from the New Kingdom era can still be viewed today. The Luxor Temple and the Karnak Temple are two other famous attractions that provide an insight into the religious beliefs and practices of ancient Egyptians. A felucca ride on the Nile River and a visit to the lively souks are also highly recommended experiences.
