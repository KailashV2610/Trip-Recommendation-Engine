As a tourist in Baku, Azerbaijan, you'll find yourself in a bustling city on the shores of the Caspian Sea, where ancient architecture and modern skyscrapers harmoniously coexist. The city offers a vibrant nightlife, with trendy bars, restaurants and clubs offering local and international cuisine, entertainment and music. You can explore the Old City, with its narrow winding streets, ancient walls, and beautiful mosques or visit the Palace of the Shirvanshahs, a historic monument showcasing the traditional Azerbaijani architecture. Baku also boasts the Flame Towers, three skyscrapers that light up at night, providing a spectacular view of the city.
