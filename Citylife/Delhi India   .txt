Delhi, the capital city of India, is a bustling metropolis that offers a rich cultural heritage, vibrant markets, and delectable cuisine. As a tourist, you'll be swayed by the city's striking contrasts between the modern and the ancient. Delhi boasts some of India's most iconic landmarks such as the Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Qutub Minar, Lotus Temple and India Gate. The city is also home to numerous museums, galleries and art centers showcasing its diverse history and culture. When it comes to food, Delhi offers some of the best street food in the world, enjoyed in places like Chandni Chowk and Khan Market. Delhi is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking to explore India's past and present.
