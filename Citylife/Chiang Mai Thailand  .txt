Chiang Mai, located in northern Thailand, is a charming city known for its rich culture, historic temples, stunning landscapes, and lively night markets. Visitors can explore ancient temples such as Wat Phra Singh, Wat Chedi Luang, and Wat Phra That Doi Suthep, or take a stroll through the colorful and bustling Night Bazaar. Adventure seekers can enjoy zip-lining, white water rafting, and elephant sanctuaries in the lush countryside. Chiang Mai's food scene is renowned for its unique northern Thai cuisine, which includes delicious dishes like khao soi and sai ua. Overall, Chiang Mai offers a delightful blend of old-world charm and modern amenities, making it a perfect destination for tourists.
