Belgrade, Serbia is a vibrant and exciting city, with a rich history and culture. As a tourist, you will be captivated by the bustling streets, lively cafes and bars, stunning architecture and impressive museums. The city is situated on the banks of the Danube River, which provides a picturesque backdrop to the many attractions, including Kalemegdan Fortress, the Cathedral of Saint Sava and the National Museum of Serbia. With a thriving nightlife scene and excellent cuisine, Belgrade is the perfect destination for tourists looking for a taste of Eastern Europe's unique and fascinating culture.
