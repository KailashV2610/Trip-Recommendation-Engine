Tbilisi, the capital of Georgia, is a city filled with rich history, culture, and breathtaking views. The winding streets of the Old Town are a labyrinth of colorful buildings, hidden courtyards, and historic landmarks. The city is surrounded by lush green mountains, with the Mtkvari River flowing through the center. The Narikala Fortress offers a stunning panoramic view of the city below. The sulfur baths of Abanotubani are a must-visit for relaxation and wellness. Delicious Georgian cuisine, known for its hearty meat dishes and fresh local produce, can be found in traditional restaurants throughout the city. Overall, Tbilisi is a charming and enchanting destination for any traveler.
