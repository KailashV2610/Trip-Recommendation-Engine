Moscow, the capital of Russia, is a dynamic city that combines modernity and history in a unique way. It boasts world-class museums, galleries, and landmarks such as the iconic Red Square, Saint Basil's Cathedral, and the Kremlin. Tourists can explore the vibrant streets, indulge in traditional Russian cuisine, and experience the local culture through theatrical performances or ballet shows at the famous Bolshoi Theatre. The city also offers a bustling nightlife scene with trendy clubs, bars, and restaurants. Beyond the city limits, visitors can take day trips to the Golden Ring cities or enjoy outdoor activities in the surrounding countryside.
