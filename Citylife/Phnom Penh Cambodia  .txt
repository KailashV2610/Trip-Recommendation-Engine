Phnom Penh is a bustling city that effortlessly blends traditional Cambodian culture with modern amenities. As a tourist, you can explore the city's rich history by visiting landmarks like the Royal Palace, National Museum, and Killing Fields memorial. The food scene is also a highlight, with delicious street food, trendy cafes, and upscale restaurants all available. Shopping is also a must-do, with local markets, boutiques, and souvenir shops dotted around the city. Despite the traffic and urban sprawl, Phnom Penh retains a distinct charm, thanks to its friendly locals and laid-back vibe.
