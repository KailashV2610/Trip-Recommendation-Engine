Agra India is a city that captures the essence of Indian culture and history. As a tourist, you cannot miss the grandeur of the iconic symbol of love, the Taj Mahal, an architectural masterpiece and a wonder of the world that showcases Mughal grandeur, intricate marblework and lavish gardens. You can also explore the grand Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site. The bustling bazaars and vibrant streets of Agra offer numerous shopping opportunities for souvenirs and local handicrafts such as leatherwork and carpets. The city's cuisine is a delicious blend of Mughal and North Indian cuisine that will tantalize your taste buds.
