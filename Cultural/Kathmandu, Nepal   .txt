Kathmandu, Nepal is a vibrant and bustling city filled with rich culture and history. As a tourist, the first thing you'll notice is the colorful buildings and traditional architecture that blends seamlessly with the modern infrastructure. The city is known for its stunning temples and shrines such as Swayambhunath Stupa and Boudhanath Stupa. The food scene is diverse and offers a variety of delicious street food and local delicacies. The people are friendly and welcoming, always willing to share their culture and traditions. The city offers a unique mix of ancient history and modern-day life, making it a must-visit destination for any traveler.
