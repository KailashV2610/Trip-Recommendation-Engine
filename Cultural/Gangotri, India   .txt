Gangotri is a small and serene town located in the Uttarkashi district of the Indian state of Uttarakhand. It is situated at an altitude of 3,048 meters above sea level, and is known for its beautiful mountains, valleys, and glaciers. The town is also famous for the Gangotri Temple, which is dedicated to the Hindu goddess Ganga. From here, you can take a trek to the Gaumukh Glacier, which is the source of the holy river Ganges. The scenic beauty of Gangotri and its surroundings is breathtaking, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.
