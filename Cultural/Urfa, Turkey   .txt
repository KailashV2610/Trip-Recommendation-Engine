Urfa, Turkey is a beautiful and historic destination for any tourist. Famous for its food, Urfa is known for its grilled meats, tasty pastries and traditional Middle Eastern dishes. The city is home to several historical sites such as the ancient castle of Urfa and the Pool of Sacred Fish. Urfa is also the birthplace of the prophet Ibrahim and boasts several religious sites like the Cave of Abraham and the Mosque of the Prophet Job. The bustling bazaar in the historic district of Urfa is a great place to indulge in shopping and explore the city's vibrant culture.
