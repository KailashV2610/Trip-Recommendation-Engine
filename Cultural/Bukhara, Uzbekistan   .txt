Bukhara is a charming city located in the heart of Uzbekistan. The city has a rich history spanning over thousands of years and is dotted with numerous Islamic architecture and cultural heritage sites. The city is surrounded by walls and has a number of bustling bazaars, vibrant silk and carpets markets, ancient madrasas, and mosques such as the iconic Kalon mosque. Visitors can relax in traditional tea houses and sample local Uzbek cuisine or take part in traditional performances. Overall, Bukhara is a cultural hub that offers a unique travel experience to tourists looking to explore Uzbekistan's rich cultural heritage.
