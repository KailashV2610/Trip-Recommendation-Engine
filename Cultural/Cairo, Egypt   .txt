Cairo, the capital city of Egypt, is a bustling metropolis that promises an exciting and unforgettable experience for tourists. The city is steeped in ancient history and offers a glimpse into its rich past with its many museums, pyramids and temples. Walking through the crowded streets, you'll find colorful markets, towering minarets and a myriad of tantalizing street food to enjoy. The Nile river flows through the city and offers scenic boat rides for tourists. You can explore the Old Cairo with its narrow alleys, ancient mosques and other historical landmarks. With friendly locals and a vibrant culture, Cairo is a must-visit destination for any traveler.
