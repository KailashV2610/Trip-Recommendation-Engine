Polonnaruwa is a historic city located in the North Central province of Sri Lanka. As a tourist, you will be amazed by the ancient structures, intricate carvings, and beautiful landscapes. The city has many UNESCO World Heritage sites, such as the Gal Vihara, which houses four beautifully carved Buddha statues. You will also be delighted by the well-preserved ruins of the Royal Palace, audience halls, and temples, which showcase the city's rich cultural heritage. The calm waters of the Parakrama Samudra reservoir and lush green forests surrounding the city provide excellent opportunities for picture-perfect moments and serene nature walks.
