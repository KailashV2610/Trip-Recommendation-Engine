Knock is a charming village in the west of Ireland, renowned for being the site of a famous apparition of the Virgin Mary in the 19th century. It's a peaceful and nostalgic location, with classic Irish architecture and welcoming locals. Visitors can explore the Knock Shrine and its stunning basilica, visit the statue-filled grounds, and light a candle for loved ones. There are also nearby historical sites like the Mayo Abbey and the ancient Rathcroghan complex. For those looking to shop and relax, the village offers local crafts, traditional cuisine, and cozy pubs, all nestled amongst the serene Irish countryside.
