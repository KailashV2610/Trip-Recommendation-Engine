Qom is a holy city located in Iran that boasts a fascinating history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. As a tourist, you are sure to be impressed by the imposing shrine of Fatima Masumeh at the city's center, which draws thousands of faithful visitors from around the world every year. The bustling bazaars are a paradise for shoppers, with endless opportunities to stock up on unique souvenirs and handicrafts. The city is a hub of learning, with several well-regarded madrasas and universities offering excellent education programs. Overall, Qom is a captivating destination for anyone interested in exploring Iran's rich cultural heritage.
