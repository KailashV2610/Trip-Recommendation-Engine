Santiago de Compostela, Spain is a charming and historic city located in the northwest region of Spain. As a tourist, you will be awed by the beauty of the city's stunning architecture, narrow streets, and charming alleyways. The main attraction of the city is the famous Cathedral of Santiago de Compostela, which sits at the center of the city's main plaza and draws thousands of pilgrims and tourists every year. In addition to the cathedral, the city has a wealth of museums, parks, and cultural institutions to explore, as well as a vibrant food scene and bustling nightlife. Whether you are a history buff or simply looking to relax and enjoy the local culture, there is something for everyone in Santiago de Compostela.
