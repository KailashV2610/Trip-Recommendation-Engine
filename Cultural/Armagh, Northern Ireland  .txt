Armagh is a city nestled in the heart of Northern Ireland, known for its rich history and cultural heritage. From the stunning Armagh Observatory to the beautiful St. Patrick's Cathedral, the city's landmarks are a testament to its extensive past. The Georgian architecture and public squares like Market Place and The Mall are perfect spots to relax and unwind. The city is also popular among foodies, with numerous charming cafes and restaurants offering local dishes such as the famous Ulster Fry. Amidst the rolling hills, Armagh is a perfect destination for walking and discovering the picturesque countryside.
