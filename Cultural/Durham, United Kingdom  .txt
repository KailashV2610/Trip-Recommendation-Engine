Durham is a charming and historic city in northeast England that offers a wealth of cultural and historical attractions for tourists. The centerpiece of the city is the stunning Durham Cathedral, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that dates back to the 11th century. Other notable sites include Durham Castle, which is now home to one of the city's colleges, and the picturesque River Wear, which runs through the heart of the city. Visitors can explore the city's winding streets and enjoy its vibrant food and drink scene, as well as attend cultural events and festivals throughout the year.
