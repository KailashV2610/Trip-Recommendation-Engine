Debre Damo is a unique and stunning Ethiopian locality that boasts a rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty. From the moment you arrive in this ancient city, you are welcomed by the warm hospitality of its friendly locals. One of its main draws is its historical monastery nestled on a plateau. Its unique rigid climb and sizeable collection of religious artifacts amazes everyone. The plateau also offers picturesque views of the surrounding landscapes with stunning rock formation, including magnificent canyons and mountains. Debre Damo also offers a chance to explore traditional Ethiopian cuisine and enjoy cultural performances from the local people.
