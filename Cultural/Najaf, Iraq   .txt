Najaf, Iraq is a fascinating city that is steeped in rich history and culture. As a tourist, you will be struck by the city's stunning architecture, which includes beautiful, ornate mosques and ancient ruins. Najaf is also a deeply religious city, with many important Shiite Muslim sites and a bustling market offering traditional goods and spices. The people are friendly and welcoming, eager to share their culture with visitors. There are plenty of delicious local foods to try, as well as entertainment options such as live music and sport. Overall, Najaf is a must-see destination for anyone interested in Middle Eastern culture and history.
