Puri is a vibrant coastal city located in the eastern Indian state of Odisha. The city is famous for its splendid beaches, ancient Hindu temples, and mouth-watering seafood. Its golden sand beaches, such as Puri Beach, provide a perfect spot for sunbathing or relaxing. The most famous temple in Puri is the Jagannath Temple, which is devoted to Lord Jagannath, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. The city also offers other attractions like the famous Sun Temple at Konark, the colorful Grand Road, and the bustling local markets. Puri is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for a delightful amalgamation of history, culture, and natural beauty.
