Fatima is a charming town located in central Portugal, best known for its deep Catholic roots and the Marian apparitions that took place here in 1917. Filled with historic buildings and religious sites, Fatima is a must-visit for pilgrims and sightseers alike. The impressive Sanctuary of Our Lady of Fatima is the centre of religious activity, drawing millions of visitors each year to its vast open-air spaces, stunning basilicas and shrines. The town itself is quaint and welcoming, with lovely cafes, shops and restaurants where visitors can indulge in local cuisine and handicrafts. The natural beauty of the region is also worth exploring, with nearby parks and nature reserves offering scenic hikes and stunning vistas.
