Haridwar is a holy city located in the northern Indian state of Uttarakhand. It holds immense spiritual significance for Hindus, who believe that taking a dip in the River Ganges here can wash away their sins. The city is filled with temples, ashrams, and ghats along the riverbank, which attract pilgrims and tourists from all over the world. The evening Ganga Aarti spectacle is a beautiful and iconic event that must not be missed. Haridwar also has beautiful natural surroundings, including Rajaji National Park, Chilla Wildlife Sanctuary, and several waterfalls. The city's vibrant markets sell everything from street food to religious items, making it a must-visit destination for a cultural experience.
