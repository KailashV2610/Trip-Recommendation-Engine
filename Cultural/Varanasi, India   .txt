Varanasi, India is a spiritual and cultural hub for tourists. It is an ancient city situated on the banks of the holy river Ganges, famous for its ghats (steps leading to the banks of the river) and temples. As a tourist, one can explore the old narrow lanes of Varanasi and witness the mesmerizing sunrise and sunset on the banks of Ganges. The city is also known for its spiritual power and offers numerous yoga and meditation centers. The bustling markets of Varanasi sell some of the finest silk fabrics, handicrafts, and traditional food. Overall, a visit to Varanasi evokes a sense of spirituality and cultural diversity.
