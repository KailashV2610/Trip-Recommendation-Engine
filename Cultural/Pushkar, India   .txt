Pushkar is a charming and spiritual town located in the Indian state of Rajasthan. The town is situated around a holy lake and is known for its numerous Hindu temples and ghats. Pushkar is a popular destination among tourists for its serene and calming atmosphere, colorful streets, bustling bazaars, and vibrant festivals. It is an important pilgrimage site as it houses the only temple dedicated to Lord Brahma, the creator of the universe. The town is also famous for its colorful camel fair that attracts people from all over the world. The place offers a wonderful mix of spirituality, culture, and heritage, making it a must-visit destination in India.
