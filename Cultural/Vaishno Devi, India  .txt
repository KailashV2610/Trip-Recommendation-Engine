Vaishno Devi, located in Jammu and Kashmir, is a popular pilgrimage site visited by tourists and devotees from across India. The shrine of Mata Vaishno Devi, nestled in the Trikuta Mountains, can be reached by trekking for about 13 kilometres from the base camp at Katra. The trek is a beautiful experience with stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. The town of Katra offers a variety of accommodation options and local markets selling souvenirs and traditional handicrafts. The atmosphere is lively and full of devotion, making it a sacred and memorable destination for tourists.
