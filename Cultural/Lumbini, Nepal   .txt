Lumbini, located in the southwest of Nepal is revered as the birthplace of Buddha, the founder of Buddhism, making it a significant pilgrimage site for Buddhists around the world. From a tourist's perspective, Lumbini offers a peaceful and spiritual atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for meditation and contemplation. The beautifully maintained monasteries and temples with ornate architecture, stunning gardens, and serene ponds, are some of the highlights of this historical site. Besides historical and religious significance, visitors can also enjoy the traditional Nepalese cuisine, shop for souvenirs, and witness the hip-hop culture that has emerged in this region.
