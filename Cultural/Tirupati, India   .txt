Tirupati is a pilgrimage city located in the southern Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. As a tourist, the main attraction of Tirupati is its world-renowned Sri Venkateswara Temple, which is dedicated to Lord Vishnu. The temple is situated on the Seven Hills, and a journey up to the temple by foot or road is a highlight of the visit. The atmosphere is lively, with shops and stalls selling souvenirs, prasadam (temple food), and flowers. Other attractions include the Sri Vari Museum, Chandragiri Fort, and the beautiful Silathoranam rock formation. Tirupati is also famed for its local delicacy, the Tirupati laddu, which is a must-try for any foodie.
