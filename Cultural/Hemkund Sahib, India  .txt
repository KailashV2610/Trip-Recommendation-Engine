Hemkund Sahib is a scenic and peaceful destination nestled in the Indian state of Uttarakhand. As a tourist, you will be greeted with astonishing views of the snow-capped Himalayan ranges, lush green forests, and glacial lakes. The serene and tranquil surroundings of the Hemkund Sahib Gurudwara make it an ideal destination for spiritual seekers. Hemkund Sahib is a revered Sikh pilgrimage site, attracting devotees from around the world, especially during the summer season. Trekking through the steep and challenging terrain followed by a dip into the sacred lake at the base of the Gurudwara is an experience not to be missed.
