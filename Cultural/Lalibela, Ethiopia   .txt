Lalibela, Ethiopia is a charming town located in the northern part of the country. It is famous for its ancient rock-hewn churches that were carved out of the ground in the 12th century. The town is surrounded by stunning landscapes and mountains that provide an excellent backdrop for amazing outdoor activities such as hiking and trekking. At night, the town is alive with music and dance as locals and visitors celebrate Ethiopian culture. The colorful markets are a hub of activity, selling everything from local handicrafts to fresh produce. The locals are friendly and welcoming, making Lalibela an unforgettable destination for any tourist.
