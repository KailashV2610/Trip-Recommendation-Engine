Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is a bustling city in the southern state of Kerala, India. As a tourist, there's a lot to see and do here - from exploring ancient temples and monuments, indulging in local cuisine, shopping for traditional handicrafts, to relaxing on the beautiful beaches. Some of the must-visit attractions include the Sri Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Napier Museum, Kovalam Beach, and Vizhinjam Fishing Harbour. The city also boasts of lush green parks and gardens, and a vibrant nightlife scene. Overall, Thiruvananthapuram offers a perfect mix of culture, history, and natural beauty that is sure to delight any traveler.
