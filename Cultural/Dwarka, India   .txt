Dwarka, India is a fascinating destination from a tourist's perspective. As one of the seven most ancient cities in India, it boasts rich cultural heritage and religious significance, particularly for Hindus as it is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Krishna. Travellers to Dwarka can explore a host of sacred temples, including the famous Dwarkadhish temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, and the elaborate Bet Dwarka temple.
