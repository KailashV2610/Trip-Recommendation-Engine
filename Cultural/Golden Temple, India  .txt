Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a breathtaking and spiritual place located in Amritsar, India. As a tourist, you will be stunned by its gleaming golden structure surrounded by a serene lake, creating a picturesque view. The complex is also home to a bustling bazaar and delicious food stalls, serving mouth-watering local dishes. The temple's atmosphere is lively and welcoming, with devotees offering prayers and singing hymns. Visitors are required to cover their heads and remove their shoes before entering the temple, and the experience of walking on the cool marble floors and admiring the intricate gold and marble work will stay with you forever.
