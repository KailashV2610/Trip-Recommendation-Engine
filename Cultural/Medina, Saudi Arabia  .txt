Medina, Saudi Arabia is a fascinating city that draws millions of tourists from around the world each year. Located in the Hejaz region of Saudi Arabia, Medina is home to some of the Muslim religion's most important and sacred sites. Among these, the most significant is Prophet's Mosque, which attracts countless pilgrims annually. Despite being a pilgrimage destination, the city offers plenty of other things to see and do, as well. From its vibrant souks to its historical landmarks and contemporary attractions, Medina has it all. Whether you're a history buff, a shopaholic or simply seeking adventure, Medina will not disappoint you.
