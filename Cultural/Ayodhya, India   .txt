Ayodhya is a beautiful and serene city located in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, India. As a tourist, you'll be mesmerized by the ancient temples and historical monuments that adorn the city's landscape. Ayodhya is known as the birthplace of Lord Rama and as a result, attracts thousands of pilgrims and tourists annually. Some of the must-visit places in Ayodhya include the Ram Janmabhoomi Temple, Hanuman Garhi, Kanak Bhawan Temple, and the Ramkatha Park. The city is also famous for its street food delicacies, so don't forget to try some lip-smacking chaats and samosas while exploring the city.
