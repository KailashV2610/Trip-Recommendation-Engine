Kandy, Sri Lanka is an incredibly charming city with amazing cultural and historical significance that attracts tourists from all over the world. The city is well known for the Temple of the Tooth, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, that houses the sacred tooth relic of Buddha. With its scenic hills, lush green landscapes, and exotic plantations, Kandy offers a unique blend of natural beauty and urbanization. Visitors can witness the traditional Kandyan dances, explore the tranquil Botanical Gardens, and take a peaceful stroll around the Kandy Lake. With delicious local food and friendly locals, Kandy is a must-visit destination for any traveler to Sri Lanka.
