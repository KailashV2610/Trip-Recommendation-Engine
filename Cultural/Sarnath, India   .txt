Sarnath is a peaceful and spiritual destination in India, located just 13 km away from the city of Varanasi. It is a significant place for Buddhists as it is believed to be where Lord Buddha gave his first teachings after attaining enlightenment. The primary attraction of Sarnath is the Dhamek Stupa, which is an impressive 43-meter tall structure built-in 500 CE. Visitors can also explore the sacred Bodhi Tree, the Chaukhandi Stupa, and several ancient ruins from the Gupta and Maurya periods. The atmosphere is serene and tranquil, making it an ideal place for those seeking introspection, meditation, and spiritual awakening.
