Assisi is a charming hilltop town located in central Italy. As a tourist, it captivates you with its stunning medieval architecture, narrow cobbled streets, and panoramic views of the Umbrian countryside. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is best known for its association with St. Francis of Assisi, and its breathtaking Basilica of San Francesco, adorned with exquisite frescos by Giotto. Visitors can also explore the Basilica of Santa Chiara, stroll through the quaint streets of the historic center, and enjoy delicious Umbrian cuisine in local restaurants. Overall, Assisi is a delightful city that offers visitors an enriching cultural and spiritual experience.
