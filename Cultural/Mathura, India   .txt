Mathura is a vibrant and historic city located in the North Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. Known for its rich cultural heritage and religious significance, Mathura is considered one of the holiest cities in India. As a tourist, you can explore ancient temples, archaeological sites, and local markets selling handicrafts and delicious street food. The Yamuna River flows through the city, adding to its serene natural beauty. The city is especially famous for its association with Lord Krishna, and you can visit many temples dedicated to him. The lively Holi celebrations draw thousands of tourists every year, making Mathura an exciting destination to visit.
