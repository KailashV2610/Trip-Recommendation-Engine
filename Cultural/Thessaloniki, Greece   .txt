Thessaloniki is a lively port city in the heart of Greece, known for its distinct cultural heritage, stunning ancient ruins and picturesque beaches. It boasts a range of attractions, including the Byzantine-era churches, sprawling museums, lively open-air markets and the iconic White Tower of Thessaloniki. The city is also famous for its lively nightlife, with plenty of bars and clubs to explore. Visitors can indulge in some delicious Greek cuisine and enjoy a variety of outdoor activities such as swimming, diving and hiking. Thessaloniki is an excellent destination for those seeking a blend of history, culture and leisurely coastal charm.
