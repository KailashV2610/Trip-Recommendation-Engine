Yamunotri is a beautiful town located in the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand, India. Known for its religious significance and natural beauty, it is a popular destination for tourists seeking tranquillity and spiritual experience. At an elevation of 3,293 meters, the town is situated amidst breathtaking mountains, freshwater streams, and dense forests, providing an ideal backdrop for trekking or taking a dip in the holy Yamuna river. The main attraction is the Yamunotri Temple, which sits at the source of the river and attracts thousands of pilgrims each year. The town also offers various adventure activities, such as camping, trekking, and river rafting, making it a must-visit destination in India.
