As a tourist in Bodh Gaya, India, the first thing you will notice is the serene and spiritual atmosphere. This is the place where Buddha achieved enlightenment and it still holds a strong influence on the region. You will be able to visit the Mahabodhi temple complex, the Bodhi tree and other sacred sites that are of great importance to Buddhists around the world. The city is also famous for its monasteries where you can experience the daily life of monks and learn about Buddhist teachings. Aside from its spiritual significance, Bodh Gaya also has great cuisine and handicrafts that are unique to the region.
