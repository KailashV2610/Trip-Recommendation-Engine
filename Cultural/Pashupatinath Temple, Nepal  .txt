Pashupatinath Temple in Nepal is one of the most sacred Hindu temples in the world. As a tourist, you will be amazed by the intricate architecture of the temple and the breathtaking surroundings. The temple is situated on the banks of the Bagmati River and is surrounded by lush forests and hills. You can witness various religious and cultural ceremonies being held in the temple, which adds to the spiritual experience. The main attraction of the temple is the Shiva Linga, believed to be one of the twelve Jyotirlingas in the world. The temple is a perfect blend of ancient architecture and spirituality, giving tourists a unique experience.
