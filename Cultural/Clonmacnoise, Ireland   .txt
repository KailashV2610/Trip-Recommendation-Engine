Clonmacnoise is a must-visit destination for any history enthusiast visiting Ireland. The historic site, located on the banks of the River Shannon, boasts a rich history dating back over 1,500 years. The ruins of the ancient monastic settlement are some of the most well-preserved in Ireland, with a selection of churches, round towers, and high crosses available to explore. The complex also houses a small museum, offering an insight into early Christian life in Ireland. The surrounding area is picture-perfect, with stunning views of the Irish countryside, making it an ideal destination for a peaceful and educational day trip.
