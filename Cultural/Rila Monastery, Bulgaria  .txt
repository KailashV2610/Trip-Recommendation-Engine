Rila Monastery, located in the Rila Mountains southwest of Sofia, is a strikingly beautiful and historic Bulgarian Orthodox monastery. As a tourist, you'll be captivated by the monastery's unique 10th-century architectural design, adorned with colorful frescoes and elaborate wood carvings. The stunning mountain backdrop and lush gardens surrounding the monastery provide a serene and tranquil atmosphere. You can explore the on-site museum, housing a collection of religious artifacts and treasures, and visit the tomb of the monastery's founder, St. Ivan Rilski. Rila Monastery is a must-see destination on any Bulgarian itinerary.
