Canterbury is a charming city located in southeast England that is rich in history and culture. The city is known for its stunning architecture, lively arts scene, and beautiful outdoor areas. Visitors flock to the impressive Canterbury Cathedral, one of the oldest and most famous churches in the country, and St. Augustine's Abbey, which dates back to the 6th century. The city's cobbled streets are home to a variety of shops, restaurants, cafes, and pubs, making it a great place to relax and soak up the local culture. From a leisurely stroll along the River Stour to exploring the city's museums and galleries, Canterbury offers something for everyone.
