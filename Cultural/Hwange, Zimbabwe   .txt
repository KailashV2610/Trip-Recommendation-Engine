Hwange, Zimbabwe is a must-visit tourist destination for nature enthusiasts. Home to the country's largest game reserve, Hwange National Park, visitors can witness an array of stunning animals in their natural habitat, including elephants, lions, giraffes, and more. The park boasts incredible diversity of flora and fauna, with over 100 mammal species and 400 bird species. There are multiple game drives and walking safaris available. For history buffs, Hwange is steeped in colonial heritage, with landmarks such as the Sandspruit Works and the railway line, which played a vital role in the country's economy. Hwange, Zimbabwe offers an awe-inspiring experience that is not to be missed.
