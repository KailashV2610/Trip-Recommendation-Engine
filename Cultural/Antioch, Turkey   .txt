Antioch, Turkey is a beautiful city with a rich history and culture. As a tourist, you will be delighted by the numerous attractions including the stunning Seleucia Pieria Beach, the ancient ruins of the Seleucid Empire, and the iconic Daphne Archeological Site. You can also explore the city's vibrant marketplaces and taste the mouth-watering traditional dishes such as kebab, baklava, and stuffed grape leaves. The breathtaking Amanos Mountains provide a picturesque backdrop to the city and offer ample opportunities for outdoor activities like hiking and trekking. Antioch also has a distinctive Ottoman and Arab influence that adds to its charm and mystique.
