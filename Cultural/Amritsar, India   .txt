Amritsar is a vibrant city in the northern Indian state of Punjab, steeped in history and culture. At the heart of the city lies the Golden Temple, a spiritual sanctuary for Sikhism, shimmering in the sun and attracting visitors from around the world. Beyond the temple, travelers can explore the bustling bazaars and markets, overflowing with colorful textiles, spices, and traditional handicrafts. Amritsar is also known for its delicious Punjabi cuisine, with street food vendors selling spicy and savory treats like chole kulche and lassi. Whether it's for the history, spirituality, or cuisine, Amritsar offers a captivating experience for any traveler.
